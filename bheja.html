TRAVEL AGENCY IN ONTARIO

In the tourism industry, the travel agency is an integral component that promotes nearly all the activities. The travel agent has full knowledge about tourist products, mode of travel, the climatic condition of a place, accommodation elements, and the specific destinations (Park, & Allen, 2018). The agency can act on behalf of the service providers and offer the required services such as advising tourists, selling packages and distributing the individual components. To avoid the growing challenges in tourism market, the travel agency must develop a strategic plan that outlines the functions and specific time frame of all the activities (Park, & Allen, 2018). The plan must contain all the features of a functional travel agency. The agents must ensure that all the features of the agency relate to all elements that make travel safe, secure, and affordable. First, managers must establish a close link between the agency and the principal suppliers to ensure that tourists get the required services in time. Second, the agent must build a good image of the principal company and perform close marketing on social media and other platforms (Park, & Allen, 2018). Through the social continuous process, the agent must provide authentic and reliable travel information available in easily accessible places. Importantly, the agent must maintain a good relationship with the clients from different parts of the world and reach them through emails to specifically thank them for choosing the principal products.
